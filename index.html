iHello and welcome to Ibiza Life!

Whether it is golden beaches, the burning sun, gorgeous warm air, beautiful blue waters, live bands, superstar DJs, clubs the size of cathedrals and crowds of tanned party goers from all over the world, Ibiza has it all.

Together with gorgeous coastlines, sunsets, boat trips and hiking opportunities, you have the recipe for one of the most appealing and diverse islands in the world.

An island in the Mediterranean Sea off the eastern coast of Spain, Ibiza is the third largest of the Balearic Islands.

Ibiza is well known for its association with nightlife, electronic dance music and for the summer club scene, all of which attract large numbers of tourists. Although it has some of the best nightlife in the world, Ibiza is a diverse holiday hotspot, and there are plenty of things to do outside of the clubs.

Ibiza has everything you need, beautiful beaches, fantastic food, unforgettable sunsets, water sports, e-biking, guided walks, and much more. We are here to help you find whatever you need, please check out the various things to do below, and contact us on the contact form at the bottom of the page with any requests!

Activities

Ibiza’s beautiful sunsets are simply legendary. Café Del Mar is one of the the best places on the island to watch the sunset.

There's lots of different boat trips available on the island. At the ferry terminal in Ibiza Town you can book a boat trip to Formentera.

There are some great hiking opportunities on the island. With amazing beautiful coastlines, and beaches, there’s around 250KM to explore!

Beaches

Portinatx in the North of the island offers three beautiful beaches, all with amazing clear waters and white sand.

Cala Llenya, on the East of the island is surrounded by pine trees and excellent turquoise waters.

Talamanca beach, near Ibiza Town offers great walks and views. There’s also a good choice of bars and restaurants.

Nightclubs

Amnesia hosts some of the biggest events on the island. There's large stacks of loud, powerful speakers and massive ice cannons to keep the clubbers cool throughout the night.

Privilege is massive - holding 10,000 people it is officially the biggest club in the world. The venue is stunning, set in a huge dome with various gardens, and back rooms.

Ushuaïa is an outside hotel and club venue in playa d'en Bossa set in beautiful surroundings, complete with amazing production, visuals and line ups.
